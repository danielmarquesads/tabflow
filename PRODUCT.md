# TabFlow

## What it is
Chrome MV3 extension that automatically closes inactive tabs after a user-defined threshold, with a local searchable archive and power-user controls. 100% free, open source, no accounts, no cloud backend.

## Who it is for
People who open dozens or hundreds of tabs and want a quieter, faster browser without losing the ability to recover closed pages.

## Core jobs
1. Close truly idle tabs on a schedule the user controls
2. Never surprise-close protected or active work
3. Recover closed tabs from a local archive
4. Give power users preview, floor counts, whitelist rules, and export

## Non-goals (v1)
- Accounts, billing, proprietary sync
- Firefox/Edge packaging (Chrome first)
- Marketing landing site

## Success criteria
- Load unpacked and work offline
- Active tab never auto-closed
- Archive restore works in one click
- Settings and archive stay on-device
